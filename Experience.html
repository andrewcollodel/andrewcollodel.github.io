Experience

My career has been a fascinating and unique journey, beginning as a construction plant mechanic and land surveyor, and progressing through roles in construction, farming, disaster management, and currently, the study of data science.

Data Science

I am deeply committed to collaborating with Adult Social Care teams to optimize their workflows, monitor progress, and ensure timely attention to all cases. Recent initiatives include the development of the Provider Portal Diagnostics Report, which tracks care provider visit uploads against contracted visits to identify discrepancies. Additionally, I have introduced the Support Plan Diagnostic Report, which performs over twenty quality assurance checks on support plans before they are finalized and processed for payment. Furthermore, I have implemented an automated Adult Social Care User Survey tool that efficiently extracts and samples the eligible population in mere seconds, reducing the process time from six weeks to just six seconds.

Experience counts when it matters the most

Over the past thirty years, I have traveled to more than fifty countries, responding to disasters and assisting those in need. In my role within the local authority where I reside, I leverage this extensive experience to comprehend and resolve complex data challenges.

Disaster Management

I possess firsthand experience in the life-saving potential of swiftly and accurately processed data. Whether in the aftermath of the floods in Mozambique, Typhoon Haiyan (known in the Philippines as Super Typhoon Yolanda), the earthquake in Nepal, or the 2014 conflict in Gaza. I have witnessed how transforming data into actionable information can save lives and provide urgent relief to those in dire need.

Data that saves lives

I possess a profound understanding of the value of data when it is transformed into information that informs management decisions. I have witnessed firsthand how having the right information, at the right time, and in the right manner, can save lives.

Surveying, farming, construction and mechanics

I am a qualified Construction Plant Mechanic and Land Surveyor, as well as an experienced farmer and builder. My achievements include constructing two Corn Soya Blend food factories in Mozambique and Angola, which produce fortified foods for school lunch programs, benefiting over half a million school children. I hold a Master’s degree in Development Studies, a Bachelor (Honours) degree in Development Studies, and a Bachelor’s degree in Human & Social Studies, specializing in Community Development with a major in Social Work. Recently, I successfully completed the Level 4 Data Analyst apprenticeship and am currently pursuing a Level 6 Data Scientist integrated degree apprenticeship at BPP University.
