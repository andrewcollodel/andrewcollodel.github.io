There are two projects in the Data Science Portfolio.

Project 1: Segmenting an online retail company's customers

The goal of this project is to conduct a market segmentation of an online retailer's market. Market segmentation is concerned with explaining and understanding the customer's behaviour. The purpose of market segmentation according to Allenby et al (2002) is to understand and describe customer behaviour to develop a focused customer engagement strategy (Allenby et al., 2002).

Project 2: Impact Evaluation: Financial Assessment an automated progress tracking and reporting system.

The Financial Assessments solution is designed to track and monitor the progress of financial assessments. The Financial Assessment Team processes an average of two thousand eight hundred assessments per annum. Manually trying to keep track of these assessments is nigh impossible The PowerBI solution tracks and monitors the progress of each assessment and determines gaps and errors in the business process. The solution includes worker worksheets to enable Support Officers and Financial Assessment Officer keep track of their workload and ensure that oldest or priority cases are handled first. The solution also includes a suite of reports for management and the Senior Leadership Team of Oxfordshire County Council
